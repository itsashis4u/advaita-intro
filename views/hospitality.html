Accommodation-

  • The participants of the Advaita'15 will be accommodated at the student's hostel in a common room.

  • Accommodation is provided by registering on our website, those who registered online will be supplied all the provender. Those who did not register on the website can be accommodated by registering at the hospi bureau by means not assuring all the provisions cited.

  • Students who want to avail the accommodation will have to pay Rs 250/- per day per person.

  • You will be furnished with a bed, a pillow, a bed sheet, and basic amenities. However, you are encouraged to carry your own blankets since it might get a little cold at night.

  • Requests for single rooms or any other complaints in this regard will not be entertained.

  • We are not responsible for your belongings ,check your belongings before you leave.

  • We take hospitality to be of extreme importance and we make certain that all our participants and guests are taken care of with extreme warmth.
